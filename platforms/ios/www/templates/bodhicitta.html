Four Immeasurable Thoughts (recite three times)

SEM-CHEN THAM-CHE DE-WA DHANG DEY-WEY
May all sentient beings possess happiness and

GYU-DHANG DHEN-PAR GYUR-CHIG.
the cause of happiness.

SEM-CHEN THAM-CHE DHUG-NGAL DHANG DHUG-NGAL GYI GYU-DHANG
May all sentient beings be free from suffering and

DREL-WER GYUR-CHIG.
the cause of suffering.

SEM-CHEN THAM-CHE DHUG-NGAL MEY-PEY DE-WA DHANG
May all sentient beings never be separated from happiness

MI DREL WER GYUR CHIG.
that knows no suffering.

SEM CHEN THAM CHE NYE RING CHHAG DANG NYI DHANG DRAL WEI
May all sentient beings remain in great equanimity beyond attachment or

TANG NYOM LA NE PAR GYUR CHIG.
aversion for anything.
