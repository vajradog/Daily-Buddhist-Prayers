Short Tara Mantra
(recite multiple times)

Om Tare Tuttare Ture Soha

Common mantras are recited multiple times.

Lama Zopa Rinpoche says,"If we recite ten malas every day, there is unbelievable purification.
Eons and eons of negative karma are purified, especially if the practice is done with bodhicitta for numberless hell beings, numberless pretas, numberless animals, numberless human beings, numberless gods and demigods to be purified and for all beings to achieve enlightenment."
