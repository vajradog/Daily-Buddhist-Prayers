Taking Refuge

SANG-GYE CHOE-DHANG TSOG KYI CHOG-NAM-LA
I take refuge in the Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha

JHANG-CHUB BHAR-DU DHAG-NEE KYAB-SU-CHI
Until I attain Enlightenment.

DHAG-GI JHIN-SOG GYI-PEY SO-NAM-KYI
By merit accumulations from practicing generosity and the other perfections

DRO-LA PHEN-CHIR SANG-GYE DRUP-PAR-SHOG
May I attain Enlightenment, for the benefit of all sentient beings.
